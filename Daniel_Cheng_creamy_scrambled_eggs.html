Creamy Scrambled Eggs

  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 eggs
  • bunch of diced chives
  • half cup creme freshe
  • tbsp butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • (optional) cayanne pepper

Directions

  1. Add oliver oil and butter in pan on low heat, crack eggs in and work with spatula.
  2. cook slowly, scraping cooked egg from the bottom and sides of the pan
  3. once eggs are no longer watery, add half of seasonings
  4. fold in the diced chives and creme freshe and remove from heat when eggs are almost cooked. (not fully cooked though!)
  5. season to taste and serve on toast!
